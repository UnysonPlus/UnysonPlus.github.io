# Build a call-to-action section

A call-to-action (CTA) is a full-width band with a heading, a line of supporting text, and a button.
Here's how to build one with the [Page Builder](/docs/page-builder).

## Steps

1. Edit a page and click **Unyson Builder**.
2. From **Layout Elements**, drag a **Section** onto the canvas. Open its options and set a
   background (Layout → Background): a brand color, a gradient, or an image.
3. Inside the section, drop a **Call To Action** element (Content Elements). It bundles a title,
   text, and button in one block. Prefer to compose it yourself? Use a **Special Heading** + **Text
   Block** + **Button** in a column instead.
4. Fill in the title, supporting text, and the button's **label** and **link**.
5. **Update**, then view the page.

## Make it stand out

- Give the section a **Min Height** and **Content Vertical Align: Center** (Layout tab) for a taller,
  hero-style CTA.
- Use a **gradient** or an image background with a color overlay for contrast.
- Keep the button label action-oriented, "Start your free trial" beats "Submit".

## Reuse it everywhere

If the same CTA appears on several pages, build it once as a
[reusable content block](./reusable-content-block.md) and embed it, then editing it once updates
every page.
